Empowering Through Workshops

Our workshops provide valuable life skills and career guidance, empowering youth with the knowledge and confidence to build a strong foundation for personal and professional success in fields like furniture design, construction, and environmental protection.

  • Life skills (e.g., time management, communication)

  • Sustainable environmental practices

Guidance and Support for Growth

We offer personalized mentorship and a strong support network to help youth navigate challenges and unlock their full potential. Through one-on-one guidance and peer groups, we help them thrive in fields such as furniture design, construction, and environmental protection.

  • One-on-one mentorship sessions

  • Guidance in career and entrepreneurial endeavors

  • Awareness campaigns to reduce stigma against youth seeking skills and opportunities

How EDI GROUP Changes Lives

At The Elite Designers and Innovators Group, we are dedicated to empowering unemployed youth by equipping them with essential skills in furniture design, construction, and environmental protection. Through hands-on training, mentorship, and community engagement, we enable them to build sustainable futures, drive innovation, and create a positive impact in their communities.

Skill-Based Training

We provide hands-on training in furniture design, construction, and environmental protection to empower unemployed youth for sustainable careers.

Mentorship Programs

One-on-one guidance and peer support groups help unemployed youth build confidence and navigate life’s challenges.

Community Outreach

Awareness campaigns work to reduce stigma and promote acceptance, fostering a supportive environment for youth.
