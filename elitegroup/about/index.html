About Us

THE ELITE DESIGNERS AND INNOVATORS GROUP is a Rwanda-based organization dedicated to empowering unemployed youth through skill development in furniture design and fabrication, construction, and environmental protection. By focusing on sustainable practices, we aim to foster innovation, economic growth, and environmental stewardship in Rwanda.

About Us

We are a group of innovators focused on developing the youth of Rwanda through hands-on training in furniture design, construction, and environmental sustainability. Our programs are designed to build essential skills for the future, equipping participants with the knowledge they need to succeed in the growing industries of Rwanda.

  • Providing valuable skills to Rwanda's youth in furniture design and fabrication.
  • Promoting sustainable construction and environmental protection practices.
  • Empowering the next generation of entrepreneurs and skilled professionals.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower Rwanda's youth with the skills they need to become successful professionals and entrepreneurs. We aim to build a sustainable future through education, innovation, and environmental responsibility.

Skill Development

We provide hands-on training in furniture design, construction, and environmental management to equip the youth with the skills necessary for the future.

Innovation

Our programs focus on fostering innovative thinking, enabling youth to find solutions to challenges in the furniture and construction industries.

Environmental Responsibility

We believe in the importance of sustainable practices in every aspect of our work, ensuring that environmental protection is central to our projects.
